A Life of Devotion & Service

Sri Sharvana Baba

Babaji was born to a humble family in the village of Sreekrishnapuram, Kerala. Swami Murali Krishna is the eighth child of Smt Lakshmi Ammal and the late Sri Krishnankutty Gupthan. Despite the definite signs of divinity from his early years, he lived a life of simplicity, devotion, and service.
