Social Impact

Sri Babaji’s global mission is founded on four pillars of charity, which include providing Food, Clothes, Medicines, Education to the underprivileged, especially in rural and tribal areas. Also, many more social service initiatives have been initiated and carried out regularly in India and Srilanka. Further, each ashram/centre overseas also performs similar charitable activities in the respective countries/regions.
